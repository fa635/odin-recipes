Spaghetti

Description

"Learned how to make it from my sister, it was a family favorite and still is. Its quick and easy more flavorful than jarred sauce."

Ingredients

  • small onion (chopped)
  • bell pepper (chopped)
  • tablespoons garlic powder
  • tablespoons butter
  • teaspoon salt
  • teaspoon pepper
  • (15 ounce) cans tomato sauce
  • (16 ounce) box spaghetti noodles

Steps

  1. On medium heat melt the butter and sautee the onion and bell peppers.
  2. Add the hamburger meat and cook until meat is well done.
  3. Add the tomato sauce, salt, pepper and garlic powder.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. CSalt, pepper and garlic powder can be adjusted to your own tastes.
  6. Cook noodles as directed.
